Follow-Up Details for Our Partnership

  • 1. Delivery & On-Site Logistics
    • "For delivery and drop-off on October 21st, who will be our primary on-site contact?"
    • "Where at the venue should we deliver the treats? Is there a specific loading dock, entrance, or a designated area to bring them directly to?"
    • "Will you need any quick assistance setting them up once they're delivered, or will your team handle the full display arrangement?"
    • "Will there be a table or display stands for us to use, or do we need to bring our own?"
  • 2. Packaging & Presentation

    Regarding how the treats will be packaged for delivery, we can offer a few options. We can deliver the individually wrapped Rice Crispy Treats, Cake Pops, and Gourmet Pretzel Rods:

    • A. Plain Bulk Boxes: Treats are securely packed in simple, unadorned cardboard boxes, ideal for efficient transport. Your team would then arrange them for display at the venue.
    • B. Bulk Boxes with Internal Trays: Treats are delivered in bulk boxes, but include disposable serving trays inside that can be easily lifted out and placed directly onto your display area.
    • C. Pre-arranged Catering Trays: Treats are artfully arranged on disposable catering trays, ready for immediate placement on your dessert table, requiring no additional setup from your team.
    • D. Windowed Display Boxes: Treats are presented in attractive boxes featuring a clear window, designed to be placed directly on your dessert table as part of the overall display.

    Which option works best for your team and the event setup?

    • "What is the color scheme or theme of the event?"
    • "Will our treats be the sole dessert, or will they be part of a larger dessert table?"
    • "Do we need to provide any labels for the different treats?"
  • 3. Venue & Access Details
    • "What are the parking and access instructions for delivery vehicles at that time?"
    • "Is there anything specific we need to know about venue access or security procedures for delivery personnel?"
    • "Will there be on-site refrigeration available for us to use?"
  • 4. Event Timeline & Final Confirmation
    • "Can you provide a high-level timeline for the event (e.g., when doors open, when dessert course is planned)?"
    • "What are the next steps on your end, and what's the best way to communicate any last-minute logistical details leading up to and on the day of the event?"
